# About Me

Hello! I am **Yuan Tsai (蔡远)**, an incoming master's student in Electronic Information at [Hohai University](https://www.hhu.edu.cn/) in Nanjing, China. I received my bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Technology from [Yanshan University](https://www.ysu.edu.cn/) in Qinhuangdao, Hebei Province.

I enjoy building practical software systems and exploring how artificial intelligence can support learning, quantitative research, developer tools, and everyday work. My recent projects cover AI agents, large language model applications, full-stack web development, data platforms, and self-hosted infrastructure.

You can find my open-source work on [GitHub](https://github.com/nothingyuancando).
